# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition and workforce demographics. It provides valuable insights into employee turnover trends across departments, job roles, salary ranges, age groups, and years at the company. The dashboard helps HR professionals make informed decisions to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends.
- Identify high-risk employee groups.
- Understand the relationship between salary and attrition.
- Evaluate attrition across departments and job roles.
- Support HR decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📂 Dataset

- **File:** `HR_Analytics.csv`
- **Source:** Sample HR Employee Dataset

The dataset contains employee information such as:

- Employee Count
- Age Group
- Gender
- Department
- Job Role
- Monthly Income
- Years at Company
- Marital Status
- Attrition Status

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Employees aged **26–35 years** show the highest attrition.
- Most attrition occurs among employees earning **Up to 5K** salary.
- Employee turnover is highest during the **early years of employment**.
- Laboratory Technicians, Sales Executives, and Research Scientists experience the highest attrition.
- The dashboard enables department-wise and gender-wise analysis using interactive slicers.
